About Us

Learn about the mission, the team, and the open-source philosophy behind the BGPS Launcher.

Our Mission

BGPS Launcher was born from a simple idea: to make accessing Blockman Go games faster, easier, and more convenient for everyone. We saw a need for a centralized, powerful tool that could serve the community, whether you're a casual player looking for fun, or a content creator needing to quickly generate shareable links. Our mission is to provide the best possible utility for the Blockman Go ecosystem, and to do it for free, forever.

Open Source & Community

We believe in the power of collaboration. BGPS Launcher is a 100% open-source project, which means its code is publicly available for anyone to view, audit, and contribute to. This transparency ensures that the launcher is safe and built with the community's best interests at heart. We welcome developers of all skill levels to help us improve and add new features.
